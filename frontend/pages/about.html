Our Mission

   Right Choice Educational & Charitable Trust is dedicated to making education accessible and impactful for every student. Based in Nagapattinam, Tamil Nadu, we work tirelessly to provide scholarships, educational guidance, and career support to deserving students.

               Under the visionary leadership of our founder Dr. L. Ramachandran, M.Tech., Ph.D., our organization has helped hundreds of students achieve their educational dreams and build successful careers.

Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.

- Nelson Mandela

What We Offer

Scholarships

          Financial support for deserving studentsfrom economically challenged backgrounds to pursue higher education.

Career Guidance

  Professional counseling and guidance to help students make informed decisions about their educational and career paths.

Workshops

    Workshops and training programs to enhance students' skills and prepare them for the competitive job market.

Digital Learning

    Access to digital resources and online learning platforms to support continuous education in the digital age.

Our Vision

Educational Excellence

  We strive to promote educational excellence by providing resources, mentorship, and guidance to students from all backgrounds.

Community Support

  We believe in strengthening communities through education and creating a network of support for underprivileged students.

Innovation & Growth

  We encourage innovative thinking and personal growth, helping students develop skills for the future job market.

Our Journey

2020

Foundation

Right Choice Educational & Charitable Trust was established by Dr. L. Ramachandran with a vision to support education in Nagapattinam.

2022

First Scholarship Program

Launched our first scholarship program supporting 25 deserving students from economically challenged backgrounds.

2023

Career Guidance Center

Established a dedicated career guidance center to help students make informed decisions about their future.

2024

Educational Workshops

Started conducting regular educational workshops and seminars to enhance students' skills and knowledge.

2025

Digital Learning Initiative

Launched digital learning initiatives to ensure education continuity during the pandemic.

2025

Expanding Horizons

Expanding our reach to more districts in Tamil Nadu and diversifying our educational support programs.
