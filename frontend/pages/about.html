About Our Trust

Empowering students through education since 2010

Our Mission

Right Choice Educational & Charitable Trust is dedicated to making education accessible and impactful for every student. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for transformation and empowerment.

Dr. L. Ramachandran, M.Tech., Ph.D.

Founder & Chairman

With over 25 years of experience in education, Dr. Ramachandran established the Right Choice Educational & Charitable Trust with a vision to bridge educational gaps and provide opportunities for deserving students from all backgrounds.

"Education is not just about academic excellence, but about creating well-rounded individuals who can contribute meaningfully to society."

Our Journey

2010

Foundation

Right Choice Educational & Charitable Trust was established in Nagapattinam with the mission to support underprivileged students.

2012

First Scholarship Program

Launched our flagship scholarship program supporting 50 students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.

2015

Career Guidance Center

Established a dedicated career guidance center to help students make informed decisions about their future.

2018

Educational Outreach

Extended our services to rural areas of Tamil Nadu, reaching over 1000 students annually.

2020

Digital Learning Initiative

Launched digital learning programs to ensure education continuity during the pandemic.

2023

Expansion

Expanded our reach to neighboring districts, impacting over 5000 students through various educational initiatives.
